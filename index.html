Our story

Real world data and clinical research are our passion! We are a team of postgraduate research professional that want to advance the way real world data is used. Find our LinkedIn bios here (link).

Our vision

Health data shouldn't be something you give freely and without oversight to companies. You should be rewarded, and should have full control of who receives what, at any moment. DHN is a new wave of data democratization!

Our technology

By connecting individual user app data and clinical data from the DHN EHR software, we create an immense data lake that can help answer the most pressing clinical research questions. We connect trialists and researchers worldwide with people interested and eligible for trials.
